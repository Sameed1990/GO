Industries we serve

Agriculture: Throughout the history of farming, we saw how agricultural technology astonishingly changed from being driven by humans to being driven by motors to being driven by data in the end. At GQ, we believe that only a customised user experience and functionality can aid in creating agricultural enterprises that are durable and long-lasting. Because of this, we focus on agricultural advances including real-time field monitoring, artificial intelligence, data analytics, and the Internet of Things. Our entire line of solutions is designed to get to the heart of any agribusiness.

We combine our cutting-edge innovative enterprise models and process excellence with domain-led robotic process automation (RPA), artificial intelligence (AI), and analytics to produce real value. We work together with clients to promote a superior end-user experience and promote agile and lean operations.

GQ leverages its expertise in the natural gas sector to boost business performance through analytics, automation, and digital transformation. We provide a broad range of intelligent solutions and services that enable seamless client experiences, lower cost-to-serve, and increase profitability.
Government: We fully comprehend the IT requirements of all levels of government (local, state, and centre). We can aid in managing any technology requirements within budget as effectively as possible using our knowledge.
