Industries we serve

Agriculture: Throughout the history of farming, we saw how agricultural technology astonishingly changed from being driven by humans to being driven by motors to being driven by data in the end. At GQ, we believe that only a customised user experience and functionality can aid in creating agricultural enterprises that are durable and long-lasting. Because of this, we focus on agricultural advances including real-time field monitoring, artificial intelligence, data analytics, and the Internet of Things. Our entire line of solutions is designed to get to the heart of any agribusiness.
Banking: GQ works in partnership with firms in the banking and FinTech industries to accelerate their journeys toward digital transformation. We combine our cutting-edge innovative enterprise models and process excellence with domain-led robotic process automation (RPA), artificial intelligence (AI), and analytics to produce real value. We work together with clients to promote a superior end-user experience and promote agile and lean operations.
Education: Utilizing cutting-edge technology to deliver unparalleled student experiences We are skilled in creating tech-enabled solutions for on-campus engagement, student self-service portals, and interactive online learning and assessment systems. We work to make the modern digital workplace a better experience for staff. Our products promote effective data sharing and management while enhancing teacher-student collaboration and communication. 
Energy: We provide IT services that keep primary production facilities, wells, rigs, solar installations, and remote areas connected. In order to meet the needs of the modern, developing energy sector, we provide complete industrial networking service solutions. GQ leverages its expertise in the natural gas sector to boost business performance through analytics, automation, and digital transformation. We provide a broad range of intelligent solutions and services that enable seamless client experiences, lower cost-to-serve, and increase profitability.
Government: We fully comprehend the IT requirements of all levels of government (local, state, and centre). We can aid in managing any technology requirements within budget as effectively as possible using our knowledge.
