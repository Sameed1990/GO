Disaster Recovery 

Downtime is no longer acceptable in today's society. If your business is down, your customers cannot contact you or get the data they need to make business decisions. In addition to the feelings of panic and loss of control, the lost revenue adds up quickly. Your reputation can also suffer significantly.

The good news is that next-generation solutions are now readily available, giving your business access to daily backups stored in numerous on-premises locations and in the cloud.

Disaster Recovery as a Service (DRaaS) allows a business to back up its IT infrastructure and data to a third-party cloud computing environment. This information is immediately available in the event of a disaster, reducing business downtime. Many IT business leaders lack the time to develop, test and put into practise disaster recovery strategies. DRaaS transfers the responsibility for disaster recovery planning from managers to disaster recovery specialists like us.

Our failover features allow you to automate the failover to a secondary site for a minimum amount of downtime and to prevent business disruption, regardless of whether your primary IT is hosted in a data centre or by a cloud provider on-premises. Replicating your mission-critical systems to a backup location will enable cost-effective failback protection.
